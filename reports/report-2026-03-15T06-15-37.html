Plot SNAP participation rate vs poverty rate across counties using the 2022 census year.

The scatter plot shows SNAP participation rate vs poverty rate across 848 US counties for 2022. There is a clear positive correlation between the two variables - counties with higher poverty rates tend to have higher SNAP participation rates. Poverty rates range from about 2.8% to 53.2%, while SNAP participation rates range from 1.2% to 58.1%. The red dashed line (y=x) shows that in most counties, SNAP participation rate is lower than the poverty rate, though some counties with very high poverty rates show SNAP participation rates that approach or exceed their poverty rates.
